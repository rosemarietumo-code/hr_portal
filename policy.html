Firm Policies & Employee Handbook

Our commitment to transparency, fairness, and professional excellence.

1. Code of Conduct

All attorneys and staff are expected to uphold the highest standards of professional ethics and integrity.

  • Treat all colleagues, clients, and court personnel with respect and professionalism
  • Maintain client confidentiality in accordance with legal ethics rules
  • Avoid conflicts of interest and disclose any potential conflicts promptly
  • Uphold the firm's commitment to diversity, equity, and inclusion

2. Attendance & Leave Policy

Working Hours

Standard office hours are Monday through Friday, 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM. Flexible arrangements may be approved by management.

Paid Time Off (PTO)

  • Attorneys: 20 days per year
  • Staff: 15 days per year
  • Unused days carry over up to 10 days annually

Parental Leave

12 weeks fully paid leave for primary caregivers; 4 weeks for secondary caregivers.

3. Remote Work & Hybrid Policy

Tumo Legal Associates supports flexible work arrangements. Attorneys may work remotely up to 2 days per week with partner approval. Staff positions have hybrid schedules as determined by department heads. All remote work must comply with data security and client confidentiality requirements.

4. Performance Evaluation & Promotion

Annual performance reviews are conducted in December. Promotions are based on:

  • Billable hours and client satisfaction
  • Professional development and CLE completion
  • Mentorship and firm citizenship
  • Pro bono contributions

5. Anti-Harassment & Non-Discrimination

Tumo Legal Associates is committed to providing a workplace free from harassment and discrimination. We prohibit discrimination based on race, color, religion, sex, national origin, age, disability, sexual orientation, gender identity, or any other protected characteristic. All complaints are investigated promptly and confidentially.

6. Technology & Data Security

  • Use firm-approved devices and software for all client work
  • Enable two-factor authentication on all accounts
  • Report any suspected security breaches immediately to IT
  • Client data must be stored on encrypted firm servers only

7. Continuing Legal Education (CLE)

All attorneys must complete the required CLE credits annually. The firm covers:

  • Registration fees for accredited programs
  • Up to $2,000 per year for external courses and certifications
  • In-house CLE sessions are offered monthly

8. Grievance & Reporting Procedure

Employees with concerns should first speak with their direct supervisor. If unresolved, contact HR at hr@tumolegal.com. All reports are treated with confidentiality and no retaliation will be tolerated.
